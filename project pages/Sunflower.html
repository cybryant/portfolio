Sunflower - Lost at Sea

Oil on canvas, 40" x 40"

This was one of the first paintings where I really felt like I was getting the hang of representing nature. While never taking formal classes other than a couple of drawing classes, I had read a number of books on oils. This I loved!

I say loved because it was washed away in a house destroyed by Hurricane Micheal in October 2018. Thankfully, a few months ago I was cleaning out some files and found a newspaper story about an art show featuring Tallahassee City employees and it featured my painting prominantly, so I have a great photo to remember it by.
