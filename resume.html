Profile

As an urban planner with 23 years of experience in state and local urban planning, I am growing my skills and experience to harness the power of Geographic Information Systems mapping and development. In particular, I want to help tackle urban growth and sustainability challenges with a focus on making technical information and complex interrelationships more accessible and comprehensible for the public so as to better inform decision making.
